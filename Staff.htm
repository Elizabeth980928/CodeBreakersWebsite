Our Staff



- Our staff are there to guide our learners reach their best version of themselves even outside the school premises. Each staff member is assigned to a certain number of students and this ensures that each student gets the time and individual attention that they need. Teachers plan and prepare teaching and learning activities, inspiring their students to change and develop their social, personal and eductaional skills.
