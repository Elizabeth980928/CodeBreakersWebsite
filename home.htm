Education

Here at Mzamomstsha Primary we aim to create classrooms where young children can thrive and cultivate their minds. We allow children to explore their imaginations and express their minds, to develop strong bonds with their peers and help each other to be smarter kids. We believe that a strong leadership from staff and parents will help young children have good role models. By providing children with the necesssary tools and skills we believe we can mold a brighter future for South Africa.
